**In 60 minutes, you are going to watch three real companies get built from nothing.**

Not slides about what AI can do. Not a recording of something that already happened. Live, on three shared screens, with AI doing the heavy lifting and three seasoned operators steering every move.

Most AI webinars are talking heads telling you what is possible. This is the rare one where you watch it actually happen, the wins, the mistakes, the recoveries, and you walk away able to do it yourself.

- **You pick what gets built.** The room votes live on the brief: an app, a company, an AI project. Nobody, including the builders, knows what it is until you choose it.

- **You judge the winner.** The room votes, and you leave with all three playbooks instead of one.

This is free. It is not lightweight. Everyone who attends gets:

1. **Three complete build playbooks.** The exact approach each founder used to go from a blank screen to a working company: how they think, what they reach for, and how they recover when AI gets it wrong. You could spend months piecing this together alone. You will watch three experts do it in an hour.
2. **The tool and prompt pack.** We send you the actual stack and the real prompts used in the session, so you can run the same plays yourself the next day.
3. **A free 15-minute 1:1 working session.** Book time with one of the operators to get unstuck on your own build. We do not lock the useful parts behind a paywall.
4. **A live vote.** Your idea could be the one three pros turn into a real company on screen.

Here is the honest math. Hiring three operators like this to build alongside you would run five figures. This costs you one hour and zero dollars, and you keep everything you learn. If you leave without at least three things you can use at work this week, we did our job wrong.
