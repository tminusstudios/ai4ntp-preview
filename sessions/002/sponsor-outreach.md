# Session 002 · Sponsor Outreach

Target sponsors, the value prop for each, and a DM template.

**Critical context:** Sponsors are upside, not load-bearing. Plan the show without them. If we land 3 of 7 commitments, the giveaways and on-stage placements get richer. If we land zero, the show ships exactly the same.

**Send outreach by end of day May 23.** Decisions land within 72 hours typically. Hard cutoff for confirming sponsor giveaways is end of day May 30 (day of dress rehearsal).

## DM template (adapt per sponsor)

Send via LinkedIn first when possible. The template:

```
Hey [first name],

Quick one for you. We are running AI4NTP Episode 002 on Tuesday June 2, a live build-in-public webinar where three founders design, build, and launch a complete startup in under an hour on stage, with AI doing the heavy lifting.

[For Tier 1: One operator is building the [brand identity in Figma / marketing site on Lovable / custom dev in Claude Code]. 15 minutes, live, in front of 70+ B2B marketers who are explicitly there to learn how AI accelerates this work.]

[For Tier 3: Justin's segment is the marketing + GTM launch — newsletter, social, email, blog, all driven by AI. Demoing your platform live is the natural fit.]

We are giving away tool subscriptions as part of the format. If you want [tool] in the giveaway pool, you get the on-stage placement free. Three things:

→ Verbal callout in the segment opener and outro
→ Live deployed URL that the audience visits during the broadcast
→ Mention in the recap page (ai4ntp.com/sessions/002) and the LinkedIn post-event social

In return we would ask for [3 Pro accounts for 3 months / whatever you want]. No cash exchange, just product-for-placement.

Replay of Episode 001 is at ai4ntp.com/sessions/001 if you want to see the format and the audience. Let me know by Tuesday May 27 if you want in.

Justin
ai4ntp.com
```

---

## Notes on negotiation

- **Lead with what you can give, not what you ask for.**
- **Be willing to take less.** Even a single Pro account giveaway lets you cite the brand on stage.
- **Don't promise more reach than you have.** AI4NTP 001 had ~30 live attendees and the recap page is growing. State the real numbers, not projections.
- **Move fast.** Most of these targets can decide in 48 hours. If you have not heard back by Friday May 29 morning, the answer is "not for this episode."
