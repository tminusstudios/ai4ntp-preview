03 — Typography

A voice with italics.

Fraunces for the headline, Instrument Serif for the aside, Söhne for the body, JetBrains Mono for the metadata. The italic is the house style — it's where the personality lives.

Instrument Serif / Accent & Italic Italic only
The italic is the brand. It's the aside, the knowing wink, the "between you and me." Used for pull quotes, episode titles, and moments where the editorial voice breaks through.
Söhne / Body 400 · 500
Our body font is a clean, warm grotesque. It reads at small sizes and has enough personality to not feel like a default. We use it for interviews, essays, interface copy, and anywhere a reader needs to spend real time. Generous line-height. Never bold where italic would do.

04 — Voice & Tone

Smart friend, not smartest person in the room.

We're the translator, not the oracle. Curious, confident, occasionally funny, never condescending. If it sounds like a LinkedIn thread, delete it.

✓ We sound like

  • "Here's what actually changed this week."
  • "The short version: it's not magic, it's autocomplete with vibes."
  • "Three things to try Monday morning."
  • "We asked a builder. Here's what they actually do."

✗ We never sound like

  • "Leveraging cutting-edge paradigms to unlock..."
  • "This changes everything. 🧵"
  • "As an expert in the AI space, I believe..."
  • "The future is here, and it's AI."
