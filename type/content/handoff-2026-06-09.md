   the shared `nav.js` (its inline header + inline countdown/menu script were deleted; form/Zoom
   script kept). `nav.js` trimmed to **Contact + Save your seat** only. One global header everywhere.

   body is currently `-apple-system`). It's a 4th webfont and isn't in the current brand stack —
   confirm against `AI4NTP-Brand-Guidelines.docx` before committing all pages to it.
5. **Exclude** `revops/`, `content/`, `mailer/`, `supabase/`, `tools/` (all vercelignored) and the
   5 dormant `assess/live/slides` pages (live-tool logic).
