About

AI for Non-Techy People.

AI4NTP is a free, live series where real operators show non-technical people exactly how they use and build with AI. No theory, no jargon, nothing held back.

What AI4NTP is

The room where you become the most AI-capable person in it.

Most AI content stops at theory. AI4NTP does the opposite. In every session, operators who actually ship share their screens and walk through the real workflows they use to get results, then hand over the exact tools and prompts so you can do it yourself the same day.

Sessions are free, live on Zoom, and recorded. Whether you have never typed a prompt or you are already automating your whole stack, you are in the right room. You can save a seat for the next session or watch a past one any time.

What we do

Show the work, then hand you the tools.

Live sessions. Operators build real things on screen, in real time, in plain language. Past builds include a full company stood up in under an hour and a teardown of how marketers actually use AI to grow.

An operator-tested tools directory. Every tool we demo live is documented with the episode and the person who used it, plus pricing and alternatives. Browse the AI tools we use on stage.

Ways to go deeper. Apply to demo as a guest or sponsor a session, or book a working session with the team.
