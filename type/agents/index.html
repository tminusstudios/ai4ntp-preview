Questions

The honest answers.

I am not technical. Is this going to go over my head?
No. That is exactly who this is built for. You describe the outcome you want in plain English. We handle every technical decision and explain what we built in language that makes sense. You will never be asked to write a line of code.
Do I actually own what you build?
Yes, completely. You own all code and all IP, outright and forever. If you ever cancel, everything we built stays yours. Nothing is rented and nothing is held hostage.
What does unlimited really mean?
You can request as many builds as you want, and they sit in your queue. We work one active build at a time so quality and turnaround stay high. Delivery depends on scope: some builds land in under 48 hours, others take more than a week, and you always know the timeline once we scope the request.
What if I do not know what to ask for?
Very common, and not a problem. Most owners start with a few obvious headaches. On the fit call we help you spot the highest-value builds you had not thought of. Knowing exactly what you need is our job, not a prerequisite.
How does the guarantee work?
We guarantee you love your first build. If for any reason you do not, you pay nothing and keep the work. You keep the build, the code, and the IP, and owe us nothing. We take the risk so you do not have to.
What does it cost, and how does scaling work?
It starts at $5,000 a month for one active build lane: we work one build at a time, with an unlimited queue behind it. Want more shipping in parallel? Add a build lane at the same rate, whenever you choose. You control it, and your price never changes without your say-so. Larger rollouts and enterprise get a custom number we set with you on the call.
What happens when something breaks or needs a tweak?
We maintain everything we build for you for as long as you are a member. Fixes and small changes do not eat your build queue, they are just part of the subscription. You never touch code, and you are never stranded with something you cannot run.
Do I pay for AI tokens and compute?
On the vast majority of builds, no. We run our own local models and infrastructure, so that is covered. If a build is unusually compute-heavy, we tell you before we start, never after.
Who is actually doing the work?
Ian, Justin, and Alec, directly, with the senior team we have built around this. Not junior contractors, not an offshore shop working from a script. The same people who run our live weekly build sessions and run their own companies on these agents.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Sensitive workloads can run on our local models and private infrastructure, so your data never has to leave for a third-party API. We scope data handling with you before we build.
Can I cancel?
Anytime. No contract, no penalty, no lock-in. Pause when your queue is empty, restart the moment it fills back up, and you keep everything we have built for you.
