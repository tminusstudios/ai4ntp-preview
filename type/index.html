FAQ

Fair questions, straight answers.

I've never really used AI. Is this for me?+

Yes. AI4NTP is built for people who are not engineers. Real operators show their actual workflows on screen, in plain language, so you can follow along even if your AI experience so far is one ChatGPT search.

If it's free, what's the catch?+

No catch. Free to attend live, and the replay is free for everyone who registers. We make our living building with these tools, so showing the work is the marketing.

Are the sessions recorded?+

Yes. Register for a session and you get the replay even if you cannot make it live. Some bonuses and giveaways are live-only though, so showing up has its perks.

How is this different from other AI content?+

No theory, no slides full of jargon. Real operators share their screens and show exactly how they use AI to get results, then hand you the tools and prompts to do it yourself.

What will I actually walk away with?+

Practical, repeatable ways to use AI in your work, plus the specific tools and prompts the people on stage use every day. You leave with something you can try the same day, not a reading list.

Do I need any special software or tools?+

No. Just show up. We name every tool we use so you can try it after, and most have free tiers.

How long are sessions, and when's the next one?+

Each live session runs about an hour. You can view our prior and upcoming live sessions any time to see what’s next.

Can I share this with my team?+

Please do. Forward it to anyone who wants to keep up with AI. The more of your team in the room, the more you take back to work.
