## Eventbrite FAQs (LIVE)

Eight FAQs configured on the event page. EB reports FAQs lift organic traffic by 8%. Ordered by conversion-objection priority.

**Q: Is this really free?**

> Yes. Free for everyone. No upsell during the session, no paywall after. Live attendees also get three giveaways drawn on the call and a free 30-minute one-on-one with any of the three founders.

**Q: What if I can't make it live? Do I get the replay?**

> Yes. Register anyway. The replay lands in your inbox the next morning, along with the transcript and every tool the operators used. The live-only giveaways and the free 30-minute one-on-one go to attendees on the call. Replay does not unlock those.

**Q: Do I need to be technical to follow along?**

> No. AI4NTP is built for operators, marketers, and founders who aren't engineers. If you can use a browser, you can follow every step. Each founder narrates the moves in plain language, and the post-event recap includes the prompts and tools so you can repeat the workflow at home.

**Q: What do I actually walk away with?**

> The brand kit (logo, palette, type system, voice rules, mockups), the website code and Lovable project link, the launch kit templates and frameworks, the prompts and tools each founder used, and a 30-minute one-on-one with Ian, Alec, or Justin if you show up live.

**Q: What tools will be demonstrated live?**

> Figma, ChatGPT, Claude, Lovable, Claude Code, Cursor, Vercel, and the AI4NTP marketing tech stack. Every tool named on stage is linked in the post-event recap with the exact prompts used.

**Q: How does the audience vote on what gets built?**

> At minute 2, a 30-second poll opens in Zoom chat. You pick the company name from three candidates and the vertical from three options. Whatever the room picks is what the operators build for the next 45 minutes. No pre-baked demos. The brief is locked live.

**Q: Can I bring my team?**

> Yes. Forward the confirmation email. Each teammate needs to register on Eventbrite so they get the Zoom link and the replay. More operators in the chat means better votes, sharper hero lines, and a stronger build.

**Q: What is AI4NTP, and how is this different from other AI webinars?**

> AI4NTP is a live show for non-technical operators learning AI. Most AI webinars are talking heads explaining what's possible. AI4NTP is three founders shipping real work in real time on stage. Episode 001 covered AI search optimization (PEO, AEO, GEO) with playbooks for ranking in LLM answers. Episode 002 builds a complete startup live in under an hour.
