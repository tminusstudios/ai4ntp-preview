Not slides about what AI could do someday. Not another "future of work" talk. Seven real agents, working in production right now, torn down live so you can build your own.

We go one agent at a time, round robin. For each of the seven you will see:

- **What it does** and the exact job it took over.

- **How you build the same.** Enough of the how that you can go start your own version.

It all runs on one simple lens: every agent has to save you time, save you money, or make you more. Get all three working together and you do not just improve, you compound.

This is free. It is not lightweight. Everyone who attends leaves with:

1. **The seven-agent teardown.** What each one is, what it replaces, and how it is put together.

Hiring operators like these to build agents alongside you runs five figures. This costs you one hour and zero dollars, and you keep everything you learn.
