# Episode 001 · Post-Event Emails

## The send for this round

One email, no segmentation. Goes to everyone on the registration list. Works whether they made the live or not. Bonus offer is the lead. Recap link is the supporting evidence.

If you want to A/B with two versions next time, the segmented alternates (attendee + no-show) are at the bottom of this file.

## Email — Unified (send this)

**Subject lines (pick one or A/B):**
- `Your 30 minutes with Alec, Ian, or me`
- `Your AI4NTP 001 30-min is open`
- `Pick your operator: 30 minutes, no catch`
- `Alec, Ian, or me. 30 minutes. You pick.`

> No limit on bookings, no upsell. Plus the full recap, transcript, FAQs, and slide deck.

## Tone notes

- No em dashes. (Hard rule from `/CLAUDE.md`.)
- No "Just wanted to check in" openers. We don't.
- No hype. The session was useful or it wasn't; the recipient gets to decide.
- Match Justin's live sign-off. "Don't be a stranger" is grounded in a real moment from the recording (Justin's close at 1:05:34) and reads warmer than corporate brand-line closes.
